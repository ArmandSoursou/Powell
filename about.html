ABOUT

Discover the underground worlds and help Rems save his brother by completing all levels. Travel through the underground garden, the mine and the underworld to get to Phil. Good luck!

SCENARIO

Throughout the game you play as Rems, a young boy with no problems until January 5, 1997. It was a sunny day despite the temperature, Rems was playing with his brother, Phil in the garden. While running to his brother, Phil suddenly slips on a patch of ice. Before Rems could recover, Phil hit his head hard against a rock. Despite all of Rems' efforts, his brother does not wake up. An imposing shadow appears in front of the two young men. This shadow surrounded by gloomy birds tears Phil from Rems' arms. With a movement of the hand, the shadow opens a breach in the ground. After only a few moments, Phil is engulfed in the depths of the earth. Rems, wanting to save his brother, jumps into the breach before it closes. He finds himself in a place he had not seen before.
